Poinciana at the Crane

  • Crane Resort, Saint Philip
  • 4 guests
  • 2 bedroom
  • 2 bathroom

Poinciana, a privately owned and managed two-bedroom Oceanview residence located within Barbados’ iconic Crane Resort.

Ideal for short luxury getaways or extended stays, this spacious villa offers full access to The Crane’s world-class amenities combined with the privacy, comfort, and flexibility of a private residence.

The space
Perched atop Barbados’ most celebrated cliffside, this spacious two-bedroom, two-bathroom oceanview villa blends timeless sophistication with relaxed island serenity.

The open-concept layout features:
*A fully equipped kitchen with premium Smeg appliances
*Elegant designer furnishings throughout
*Floor-to-ceiling glass doors opening onto a private oceanview balcony

Designed for comfort and flow, the space works equally well for couples, friends, or relaxed family stays.

Guest access
Guests enjoy a seamless and well-supported arrival, including:
*Complimentary roundtrip airport transfers
*A personal meet-and-greet by your dedicated concierge
*A welcome kit on arrival to help you settle in comfortably

ENHANCE YOUR STAY
For guests wishing to elevate their experience, a selection of optional services and thoughtfully curated island experiences can be arranged upon request through your concierge.

These may include:
*Private chauffeuring and premium vehicle access
*In-villa private chef experiences
*Car rental coordination
*Pre-arrival grocery and wine stocking
*Island excursions, wellness experiences, and cultural activities

RESORT ACCESS
Guests enjoy full access to The Crane Resort’s amenities, including:
*Multiple outdoor swimming pools
*Restaurants and bars
*Tennis and pickleball courts
*Yoga classes
*24-hour Fitness Centre
*The world-renowned Crane Beach
*Golf simulator
*Kids Club and on-site cinema

Your concierge is available to assist with arrival, orientation, and recommendations throughout your stay.

Other things to note
Privately owned and professionally managed luxury residence within The Crane Resort, with full resort access and personalized guest support throughout your stay.
