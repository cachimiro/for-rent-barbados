Payment Terms

A 50% deposit is required to confirm the booking. The remaining balance is due 30 days before arrival. For bookings made within 30 days of arrival, full payment is required at the time of booking.

Cancellation Policy

Cancellations made more than 30 days before check-in receive a full refund of the deposit minus a 10% administration fee. Cancellations within 30 days of check-in are non-refundable. No-shows are charged the full booking amount.

House Rules
